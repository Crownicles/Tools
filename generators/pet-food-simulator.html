Logic:
t < feedDelay: 10% (Not hungry yet)
feedDelay <= t <= 3 * feedDelay: 100% (Hungry & Motivated)
t > 3 * feedDelay: Linear Decay (Starving)
Decay Slope = Force * 0.003
Min Probability = 10%

How to read this chart:

  • X-Axis: Time since last fed (Hours).
  • Y-Axis: Probability of finding food.
  • Blue Line: The probability curve.
  • Red Dashed Line: Feed Delay (Hungry Start).
  • Green Dashed Line: 3x Feed Delay (Starving Start).
